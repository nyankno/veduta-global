    bauxite

    bauxite is an aluminium ore and is the main source Bof aluminium. This form of rock consists mostly of the minerals, Gibbsite, Boehmite and Diaspore mixed with two iron oxides Goethite and Hematite, the clay mineral Kaolinite, and small amounts of Anatase (TiO2). The largest bauxite producing countries are Australia, China, Brazil, Guinea and India.
