cement

portland cement is the most common type of Pcement in general use around the world. It is a fine powder produced by grinding Portland cement clinker (more than 90%), a limited amount of calcium sulfate (which controls the set time) and up to 5% minor constituents. Portland cement is most often used in making concrete and mortar, which are used for construction purposes. The largest cement producing countries are China, India and the USA.
