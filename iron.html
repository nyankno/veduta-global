    iron ore

    iron ore is the primary raw material used in the I product ion of steel and — combined with steel — Constitutes the world's second largest commodity bloc by value, after crude oil. Iron ore is categorized by dint of where it is produced and priced, and by its ferrous content, expressed as a percentage. The largest iron ore producing countries are Australia, Brazil, India, South Africa and China.
