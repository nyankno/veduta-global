LITHIUM ORE SPODUMENE

lithium is an element valuable for the production of glass, aluminium products and batteries. It is mined from ores of petalite, lepidolite and spodumene. Veduta Global through its network of mines and partners across these three African countries of Nigeria, Zimbabwe and DRC are able to supply its clients' requirements.

Lithium is a highly sought-after mineral that is used in batteries, electric vehicles, and energy storage systems. As the world moves towards a more sustainable and renewable energy future, the demand for lithium is expected to increase significantly. Veduta Global has a strong network of suppliers and partners in the lithium mining industry, which allows the company to source high-quality lithium at competitive prices. Additionally, Veduta Global provides financing and other value-added services to its clients to ensure that they have access to the capital and resources needed to succeed in the fast-growing lithium market. Whether you are a battery manufacturer, EV maker, or energy storage provider, Veduta Global can help you meet your lithium needs and achieve your business goals.
